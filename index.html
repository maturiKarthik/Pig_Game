Instruction :

The game of Pig is a very simple jeopardy dice game in which two players race to reach 100 points. Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player holds and scores the sum of the rolls (i.e. the turn total). At any time during a player's turn, the player is faced with two decisions:

  • You have the liberty to set your own virtual score. (ex: 20,40,50 etc )
  • If the player rolls a 1 the player scores nothing and it becomes the opponent's turn.
  • If the player rolls 2 - 6 the number is added to the player's turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • hold Key - The turn total is added to the player's score and it becomes the opponent's turn.
